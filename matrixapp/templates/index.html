This website is designed to help you through your studies of Linear Algebra. Although it may be used solely to solve problems, the solutions are also supplemented with detailed explanations and a step-by-step flow. The main source of information for the methods and explanations is Dr. David Lay's "Linear Algebra and its Applications." There is a quickstart here, on the home page, or you may delve into individual topics through the navigation bar:

  1. Basic Operations: Add matrices, Subtract matrices, Multiply matrices, Raise matrices to power
  2. Gaussian Elimination
  3. Inverse Matrix: Inverse, Determinants
  4. Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors: Find eigenvectors and eigenvalues, Diagonalize a matrix
  5. Orthogonal Projections: Project onto a plane, QR Factorization, Gram Schmidt
